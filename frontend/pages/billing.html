Billing Processes

Manage your billing efficiently with our streamlined process for products and services.

Billing Steps

  1. Select the products or services to be billed.
  2. Verify the quantities and prices.
  3. Apply any discounts or promotions.
  4. Generate the invoice with detailed billing information.
  5. Process payment through available payment methods.
  6. Send receipt and confirmation to the customer.
  7. Maintain records for accounting and reporting.

Billing Features

  • Easy product and service selection
  • Accurate pricing and discount application
  • Multiple payment options
  • Automated invoice generation
  • Secure payment processing
  • Comprehensive billing reports
